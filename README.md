# Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac-toe, noughts and crosses, or Xs and Os is a game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3√ó3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner. <a hreh='https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tic-tac-toe'>see more...</a>
